Cued Music · private beta

The whole world of music, tuned to your taste — for every moment.

Cued is a music guide for Apple Music. Tell it the moment, and it answers with five songs picked for you — favorites you forgot you loved, next to new music that actually fits.

You liked five hundred albums. You hear the same forty songs.

Streaming apps lean on what you played recently, so last month's habits pick next month's music. The library you spent years building goes quiet, and discovery drifts toward whatever everyone else is playing.

Cued starts from everything you've ever loved and lets the moment decide what surfaces.

Buried favorites, back

The album you wore out in 2019 shows up at the right moment — not by shuffle luck.

Discovery that fits

New artists and deep cuts chosen because they sit next to your taste, not because they're charting.

Your words, your meaning

How a session goes

01

Get the beta

Cued is on TestFlight. You'll need an iPhone and an active Apple Music subscription.

02

Connect your library

Sign in with Apple and link Apple Music. The albums and songs you've liked become the starting map of your taste.

03

Say the moment

Type anything — gym sunday morning backyard hang — or tap a preset. Made-up words welcome; it figures them out with you.

04

Play the pack

Five songs, full tracks, played through Apple Music itself. Keep what lands, skip what doesn't.

05

Watch it sharpen

Each pack learns from the last one. Each time you return, it starts where you left off instead of starting over.
