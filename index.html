Cued is a music guide for Apple Music. Tell it the moment — a morning emo run, cooking jazz, the gym — and it answers five songs at a time: favorites you forgot you loved, next to music you've never met.

I Define your taste

Your library is the map. It expands like one.

Connect Apple Music and years of your own choices become the raw material — what you love, and what you've buried. One record you played to death isn't a memory; it's a door.

III Explore the world

Radio from any song. Discovery that actually fits.

Start a station from the one song you can't let go of. It plays first, then Cued follows the thread — past the obvious hits, into deep cuts, tuned by your taste. Keep what lands, skip what doesn't: every choice sharpens the next five.
